Vitamin Ventures


Vitamin Ventures was founded to make lives better with their vitamins. The company manufactures and sells vitamins directly to consumers online and to third party wholesalers. They are currently in their 5th year of business, and they know they are growing astronomically, but they want to position themeselves to be a lasting brand

We have been given 2 sets of data to study their current trends. Our team will break down each product and examine the quantity and dollar sales by month, and we will further look at each channel to see where the company should sell next. The data also provides us with customer locations, so we can guage where the brand has opportunity.
