Company Overview


Overall, total net sales for Vitamin Ventures is $8.6 million for the year 2021.
Vitamin Ventures experienced an increase in net sales over 2020 by a whopping 95%.
This substantial growth is due in large part to the company’s initiative to enter new markets such as bigbox retailers like Walmart and e-commerce sellers like Etsy.
However, the company’s website sales remain the largest contributor representing 55% of total net sales in 2021.
